DIY Virtual Reality Glasses

Materials needed:


  • Cardboard Shoe Box
  • Scissors and/or Xacto Knife
  • 2 45 mm Biconvex Lenses
  • 4 strips of Velcro
  • Gluestick

Making VR Goggles


  1. First, print out the template from the pdf file below.
    2. VR Goggles Template
  2. Second, use the scissors and the Xacto knife to cut out the templates after placing the template over the cardboard.
  3. Remember to layout the cutouts and line up the edges, using glue/velcro/other connective material to secure the goggles.

  4. Make sure mobile device can be secure, do not use glue to attach the device.

  5. Download a VR application from your mobile device's app store.
  6. Have fun!
